Our Services: Scope of Work and Customer Guidelines

Our professional moving team will arrive at your move from location on the appointed day, along with all packing materials as per the requirement. Our move supervisor will introduce himself and his crew, and after an inspection of the goods to be moved, he will give the go-ahead to the Packers to do their wonders.

We will dismantle all furniture items as per the requirement and we will pack everything and wrap the goods according to local move standards. Goods packing, by the nature of the move and distance involved, whilst maintaining and maximizing the protection of your goods, we use a combination of carton boxes, corrugated sheets, cardboard wrapping sheet, blankets, and stretch wrap for furniture items.

Once the packing is completed, our crew will commence to load the items onto our covered vehicle and transport to your desired location.

In the new house, we will unpack and reassemble only the items that we dismantle from the move-out location into the move-in location. Please note that our scope of work does not include fittings or assembly of any new items that were not transported by our team during the move.

The furniture and other items will be placed as per your requirements and we will provide you the white goods connection service.

Upon completion of the job, we will collect and dispose of all unwanted packing debris. If you want to keep the boxes for a couple of days for you to unpack, you can have them and call us when you are done so that we may collect it on a mutual date and time.

Our team also confirms at the end if there is any pending task left or any damage. Please Note: In case of any damage, the customer needs to report the customer support team immediately on the move day before the move team leaves the customer location in order to qualify for The Giant Movers complimentary insurance policy. Any damage reported afterwards, we will not be responsible for that damage and it cannot be claimed.

Operational Hours

The Giant Movers support channel is available all throughout the year and our Move operations are available and operational on weekends and most public holidays. The Giant Movers move operations may not be available during the Eid Holidays in UAE. In the event of any bookings during the Eid Holidays, it will be communicated prior to ensure smooth move operation.

Complementary Damage Protection

Every precaution is taken to ensure there are no damages done during any move, painting job, and/or during the duration of your furniture and other items stored through The Giant Movers. In the unlikely event of any such incident in which there is some damage to the furniture items or any other personal belongings, The Giant Movers offers our Complimentary Insurance which gives the customer AED 5/KG (weight of the item damaged).

Additional Truck/Material Requirements

Any of the Moving Jobs which are booked without a survey and/or it is booked online by the customer is based on a standard inventory selected at the time of booking. In the event another truck is required to handle the additional furniture items and/or boxes, AED 400 is chargeable per additional truck and AED 10 per additional box required to complete the job.

Movement of Furniture through Stairs

In the event where the furniture or other move related items are too big to be moved through the elevator and have to be carried through the stairs, there is an applicable charge of AED 20/Item per floor.

It’s all about Respect

The Giant Movers and its team ensure to provide due respect to its valuable customers, and if there is any disrespect shown, please feel free to let us know and the concerned person will be dealt with strictly as per company policy. We expect the same from our valuable customers to give respect to our team members who are doing their best to ensure a hassle-free move experience for you. If any abuse or disrespect is shown, The Giant Movers reserves the right to stop/cancel the move immediately and to report the incidence to local authorities.
